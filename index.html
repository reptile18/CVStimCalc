Coronavirus Stimulus Summary

  • Income Thresholds are:
    • $75,000.00 for Individuals
    • $112,500.00 for Head of household
    • $150,000.00 for Married couples
  • For individuals making more than the threshold, the Stimulus check will be $5 less for ever $100 above the threshold
  • For individuals making over $99,000.00, head of households making over $136,500.00, and married couples making over $198,000.00, checks will not be sent, but affected parties can still claim dependent children.
  • For every child dependent under the age of 17, an additional $500 will be included.
